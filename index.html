Show Segments

Best vehicle for you

Watch a consumer/contestant find the perfect car for their needs. The guest is interviewed by our host, car guy, former on-screen talent with "Meguiar's Car Crazy" and comedian -- with live appearances at "The Laughie Taffie" in Lemur, KS. They describe their needs, test drive cars chosen by the host, and then they reveal their automobilic future!

Fix up 'Soop up

Soup Up (v, phrase) alt. Soop Up, Supe Up. A verb meaning to modify something to increase its power, efficiency, or impressiveness. That's about right. We make something better, starting with a customer's or dealer's vehicle.

Auto Love

A segment dedicated to the beauty and wonder of all things auto. We'll dive into one woman's (or man's) quest to secure that barn find; we'll follow up on our car choice contestants and the vehicle purchased; we'll showcase the new and novel.
